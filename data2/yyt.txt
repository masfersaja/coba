Create RTF with template brackets inside Text like "Dear, {{{name}}}, how are you?"

Open the file and load the content with $content=file_get_contents('path_to_file');

Replace the text as you wish: $content=str_replace('{{{name}}}', 'Bruce Wanye', $content);

Save the content to a new file: file_put_contents('path_to_file2', $content);

Now convert to PDF: excec("soffice --headless --convert-to pdf 'path_to_file2' --outdir 'target_directory'");
